Growing Up In Maine

I was born on August 1st 2001 in Bangor Maine. I lived in a Bangor apartment for my first 3 years untill we moved to Waterville. Growing up, my mom ran

daycare through our house, so I was always around other kids. Growing up in Maine, we were always outside doing something. whether that was riding our

bikes around the neighborhood, exploring the woods around our house, or even hunting squirrels in the backyard.
